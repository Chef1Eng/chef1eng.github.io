Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Solutions Flames provides AI-powered chatbot automation services for Instagram and WhatsApp.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our services.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Our chatbot interactions happen in real-time, directly through official platform APIs.

2. What Information We Access

When you connect your Instagram or WhatsApp account, our chatbots may access:

  • Basic profile information (username, user ID).
  • Public messages and interactions sent to your account.

We do NOT collect:

  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses
  • Device details
  • Browsing behavior
  • Analytics data
  • Payment information

3. How We Use This Information

  • Provide automated chatbot responses.
  • Enhance user engagement and streamline interactions.
  • Improve customer support efficiency.

We do NOT:

  • Store user data after interaction processing.
  • Sell or share data with third parties.
  • Track users across platforms.

4. Data Retention & Security

  • Since we do not store user data, there is no long-term retention.
  • All chatbot interactions are handled securely in real-time.
  • Our integrations use secure OAuth 2.0 protocols.

5. Third-Party Services

Our services integrate directly with Instagram and WhatsApp APIs. We do not control these platforms or their data policies.

Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • WhatsApp Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect or process any child-related data.
